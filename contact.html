Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. For urgent matters, please indicate "URGENT" in your subject line or call our office directly during business hours.

Can I submit news articles or event information from my region?

Yes! We welcome news submissions from all AFA members across Africa. Please use the "News Submission" option in the inquiry type dropdown above. All submissions are reviewed by our editorial team and may be featured on our website or in our newsletter.

Do you provide French language support?

Yes, we are a bilingual organization. You can select French as your preferred language in the contact form, and we will respond in French. Many of our resources are also available in both English and French versions.

How can I become a member of AFA?

Membership is open to all Augustinian communities, institutions, and individuals across Africa. Please select "Membership Information" as your inquiry type, and we will send you the membership application package with detailed instructions.

Where should I send my donation or financial contribution?

For donation inquiries, please select "Donation Question" as your inquiry type. We will provide you with secure payment options, bank details, and information about how your contribution supports our mission across Africa.
